Project Description:
------
This project is funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, with Prof. Weimin ZHUANG as the Principal Investigator. It focuses on design strategies for urban existing spaces and their impacts, and, through the development of an evaluation framework and the conduct of empirical studies, provides evidence-based recommendations to support data-driven and rational renewal of urban spaces.
