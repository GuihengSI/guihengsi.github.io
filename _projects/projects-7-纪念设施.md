Project Description:
------
This project is funded by the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of China, with Prof. Weimin ZHUANG as the Principal Investigator. It synthesizes detailed control approaches and approval procedures for the major memorial facilities in developed countries, with the aim of providing reference and support for the formulation of relevant regulations and guidelines in China.
