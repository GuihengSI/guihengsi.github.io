Project Description:
------
This project is funded by the Ministry of Education, Singapore, with Prof. Chye Kiang HENG as the Principal Investigator. It examines the spatial patterns of daily mobility among older adults in public housing neighborhoods in Singapore using GPS, and develops a quantitative framework to investigate how neighborhood environmental characteristics influence these mobility behaviors.
